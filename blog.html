Purpose

The DevAcademy curriculum for the Foundations module is divided into 5 weekly 'sprints'.

As I work my way through the course work, I'll be using my blog to reflect on what I've learnt and drill more deeply into certain topics. Some of the relections will be about technical concepts, while others will be about 'core' concepts, which are where we do some exploration about ourselves and the learning process.

I hope you find them interesting and enjoy reading them!
